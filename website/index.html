The name Aequis is drawn from Latin aequus — meaning equal, fair, and just. It is the same root that gave us equity, equitable, and the very word equality. We believe the law, practised rightly, is the surest instrument through which that standard of fairness reaches those who need it most.

Aequis Legal is a litigation-first practice, built deliberately lean. We do not spread ourselves thin. Our work is concentrated where it is most consequential — the Supreme Court of India, the Delhi and Allahabad High Courts, and international arbitral tribunals. This focus is a choice, not a limitation.

We represent individuals, corporations and government institutions with equal rigour. Our practice spans constitutional law, civil litigation, commercial arbitration, corporate and insolvency law, service law, and criminal defence. What unifies our work is method: every matter is researched to its foundation, argued with precision, and handled with absolute professional integrity.

Aequis Legal is not the largest firm in the room. We are, however, among the most prepared. For clients who understand that the difference between a good advocate and a great one is the quality of thought that precedes the argument — that is the standard we hold ourselves to.
